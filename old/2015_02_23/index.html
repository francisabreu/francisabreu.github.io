Welcome!

I am a PhD candidate at the department of Biostatistics at the Johns Hopkins Bloomberg School of Public Health.

Aside from a brief period in my childhood when I wanted to be a marine biologist, I can only remember ever wanting to do one thing: statistics. I have been in this field for the past 15 years, first as a student, then as a TA, a teacher, a tutor, a consultant and a researcher.

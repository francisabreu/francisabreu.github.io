I love teaching.

I have to confess I wasn't drawn to it initially. But when the School of Statistics at Universidad Central de Venezuela needed an instructor for its Statistical Inference class, I agreed to help for one semester, as a favor to my mentor.

And that changed everything.

Ever since that class, I have been teaching and training to become a better teacher. It's been quite the journey and each course and student has helped me become a better statistician, researcher and person.

If you want to know more, check out the teaching, mentoring and certificate sections of my CV. Also, check out my teaching philosophy, and selected student comments I have gotten over the years.
